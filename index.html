about the restauraunt

Magical atmosphere, impeccable service, exquisite interior and, most most importantly, the richest selection of delicious dishes of excellent quality - these are the characteristics attract guests to our restaurant

The waiters of this establishment know all the subtleties of cooking, they will suggest to the guests the most suitable dishes for them. Regular customers are well known here, skillfully catering to their tastes.

The restaurant is ideally complemented by a cozy coffee shop located on the ground floor. A cup of amazing coffee and sweets from a local pastry chef will energize you with cheerfulness and positive energy.
